What is Little Fluffers

Little Fluffers is a place to create and maintain a health and care diary for your pets, and to easily export and share essential information with anyone who may need to look after your pets temporarily.


If you are going on holiday, or in the event of an emergency, a temporary carer will have access to a simple info card to make sure your pet receives the care it needs in your absence.


Who is this service for?

Any pet owner! For exotics keepers to share essential care information (because how many people know how to look after a Jackson's chameleon?), or to keep updated on any medical requirements a pet may have.

Little Fluffers can also be used as a diary to keep track of the health of your pets throughout their lives.
